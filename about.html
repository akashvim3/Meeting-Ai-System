About Us

We're On a Mission to Transform Meetings

Empowering teams worldwide to make every meeting more productive, collaborative, and actionable through cutting-edge AI technology.

Our Mission

Building the Future of Meeting Intelligence

At MeetingAI, we believe that meetings are the cornerstone of collaboration, but they often lack proper documentation and follow-through. Our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence to transform every meeting into a source of actionable insights and measurable outcomes.

Founded in 2023 by a team of AI researchers and productivity enthusiasts, we've helped over 50,000 users across 120+ countries save millions of hours and improve team collaboration.

We're committed to continuous innovation, ensuring our platform stays at the forefront of AI technology while remaining accessible and user-friendly for teams of all sizes.
