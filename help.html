FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start recording a meeting?
Click the "New Meeting" button in your dashboard, allow microphone access when prompted, and click "Start Recording". The AI will begin transcribing in real-time automatically.
What languages are supported?
MeetingAI supports 30+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and more. The system automatically detects the language being spoken.
Can I integrate with Zoom or Teams?
Yes! MeetingAI integrates seamlessly with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and other popular video conferencing platforms. Connect your accounts in the Integrations settings.
How accurate is the transcription?
Our AI-powered transcription achieves 98% accuracy in optimal conditions. Accuracy may vary based on audio quality, background noise, and accents. You can always edit transcripts manually if needed.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted end-to-end, and we never share your meeting content with third parties. You have full control over your data.
Can I export transcripts and summaries?
Yes! You can export your transcripts and summaries in multiple formats including PDF, DOCX, TXT, and JSON. You can also share them directly with team members or integrate with project management tools.
What's included in the free trial?
The 14-day free trial includes full access to all Professional plan features: unlimited meetings, advanced transcription, AI summaries, action item tracking, integrations, and priority support. No credit card required.
How do action items work?
Our AI automatically identifies tasks and action items mentioned during meetings, assigns them to the appropriate team members, and tracks completion status. You can also manually add or edit action items.
